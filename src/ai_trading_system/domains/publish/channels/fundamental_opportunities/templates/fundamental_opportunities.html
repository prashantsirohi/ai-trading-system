Classification Flow

  1. Collect inputsSales, EBITDA/OPM, PAT, debt, equity, CFO, capex, dividends, ROCE, ROE, and valuation fields.
  2. Compute derived metricsRevenue CAGR, PAT CAGR, D/E, FCF/PAT, OPM trend, and valuation versus history.
  3. Apply exclusion filter firstRed flags are routed before bucket classification.
  4. Apply bucket rules in priority orderQuality, High Growth, Dividend/Cash Cow, Turnaround, Deep Value, then Cyclical.
  5. Resolve overlapPrimary signal dominates; secondary buckets become tags.
  6. Review edge casesCyclicals, low-base recoveries, and insufficient valuation history require manual review.
